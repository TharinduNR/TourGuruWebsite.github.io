Research Problem

Given the literature identification on its domain related problem as follows.

  • The identification on delivery of attractions with mobile for a tourist
  • Concern on the delivery on the necessity o information with real travel experience.
  • To help on the subject of navigation and identification on attraction importance to the user.


    • Research Objectives

  • To implement a suitable mobile solution for alerting on the tourist attractions or nearby Point of Interests.
  • To route a tourist on attractions or nearby Point of Interests.
  • To deliver an visualization on localization or relative placement on tourist on attractions or nearby Point of Interests.
  • To deliver an visualization on Specific attraction(Monument or a Establishment.

    • Methodology

    By accommodating the agile practices with Mobile Software Development Life-Cycle .

    Technologies

    Development technologies are as follows.

    Flutter

    An Cross-Platform native moble app evelopment framework

    With the native IOS/Android mobile app the user will able to better experience User Interactions and gain worthwhile experience.


    Google Cloud Platform

    Accommodates map based interactions and machine learning models CI/CD

    Route generation will use an machine learning model to deliver attraction based route.
    Alerting for the nearby attractions are presented with a narration of a summary on detail generated with a Deep Learning model.


    Wikitude Engine

    Empowers the device with Geo AR and Object Tracking.

    Visualization on the nearby attractions and specific point of interests through AR object modeling.

    Firebase

    Delivers crud operation on the firebase database/storage and deployed cloud functions

    Visualization on the nearby attractions and specific point of interests through AR object modeling.
